# 800VDC Power Delivery: Component and BOM Deep Dive

As AI data centers transition to 800VDC architectures (e.g., OCP Diablo 400 and NVIDIA NVL72 Kyber racks), the physical makeup of the power supply unit fundamentally changes. To hit required power densities (often >2,500 W/in³) and efficiencies (>98%), standard silicon-based components are replaced by Wide-Bandgap (WBG) semiconductors and highly specialized passive components.

This document breaks down the Bill of Materials (BOM) for 800VDC-to-48V (and 48V-to-1V) power conversion stages, identifying both the core components and the niche suppliers driving this ecosystem.

---

## 1. Core Power Conversion (LLC DC-DC Stages)
The LLC resonant converter is the workhorse of 800VDC architectures, allowing for Zero Voltage Switching (ZVS) which drastically reduces switching losses at high frequencies (1 MHz+).

### Primary-Side Switching: GaN and SiC
To handle 800V input while switching fast enough to shrink magnetic components, the primary side relies on WBG semiconductors.
* **Gallium Nitride (GaN):** Heavily favored for high-frequency LLC stages because it lacks reverse-recovery losses and has incredibly low gate capacitance.
  * *Components:* 650V GaN FETs (used in stacked or multi-level topologies) and emerging 1250V GaN HEMTs (allowing direct conversion without complex topologies).
  * *Suppliers:* **Navitas Semiconductor** (GaNFast ICs), **Power Integrations** (PowiGaN), **EPC (Efficient Power Conversion)**.
* **Silicon Carbide (SiC):** Used for robustness, particularly in active front-end rectifiers or PFC (Power Factor Correction) stages handling the raw grid AC before it hits the 800V bus.
  * *Components:* 1200V SiC MOSFETs.
  * *Suppliers:* **Wolfspeed**, **STMicroelectronics**, **Infineon**.

### Secondary-Side Synchronous Rectification
Once the transformer steps the voltage down to 48V/54V, low-voltage switches convert the high-frequency AC back to DC.
* *Components:* 40V to 100V eGaN FETs or ultra-low RDS(on) Silicon MOSFETs. These must handle immense current (hundreds of amps) with near-zero conduction loss.
* *Suppliers:* **EPC**, **Texas Instruments**.

## 2. The "Hidden" BOM: Magnetics, Passives, and Silicon

### Controllers and Gate Drivers
High-frequency ZVS requires extreme precision in timing. Analog controllers are too slow.
* **Digital Power MCUs:** High-performance microcontrollers with high-resolution PWM timers handle the complex LLC and DCX (DC Transformer) control loops.
  * *Suppliers:* **STMicroelectronics** (STM32G4 series), **Texas Instruments** (C2000 series).
* **Isolated Gate Drivers:** Required to safely drive the high-voltage GaN/SiC gates across the isolation barrier.
  * *Suppliers:* **Silicon Labs**, **STMicroelectronics** (STGAP series).

### Magnetics
Traditional wire-wound transformers are too bulky and thermally inefficient for dense AI power shelves.
* **Planar Matrix Transformers:** These use the PCB copper traces as the windings, integrated directly into the circuit board. This allows for a very low profile, excellent thermal dissipation into a cold plate, and repeatable parasitic characteristics essential for high-frequency LLC tuning.

### Capacitors and Diodes
* **High-Voltage MLCCs (Multi-Layer Ceramic Capacitors):** Used for snubber circuits across the fast-switching GaN FETs to absorb voltage spikes.
* **Electrolytic / Film Capacitors:** Used on the 800V DC link to buffer voltage ripple. Aluminum electrolytic are common, but high-temperature film capacitors are increasingly used for reliability.
* **SiC Schottky Diodes:** Used in PFC stages and secondary rectification for their zero reverse-recovery current.

---

## 3. Power Path, Traces, and Protection

### Printed Circuit Boards (PCBs)
* **Heavy Copper Traces:** Moving hundreds of amps at 48V (and thousands of amps at 1V Point-of-Load) requires PCBs with 4oz to 6oz copper pours. Standard 1oz copper would instantly vaporize.

### Fuses and Breakers
* **Solid-State Circuit Breakers (SSCBs):** Interrupting 800VDC mechanically creates a sustained arc. Facilities rely on SiC/GaN-based SSCBs to break the circuit electronically in microseconds.
  * *Suppliers:* **ABB** (SACE Infinitus), **LS Electric**.

---

## 4. Niche & Highly Specialized Ecosystem Suppliers

Beyond the converters, operating an 800VDC liquid-cooled rack requires a periphery of highly specialized components.

### Precision Current Measurement & DC Power Analyzers
To balance the 800VDC bus and prevent AI load spikes from tripping the system, telemetry must be accurate to within 0.5% and completely immune to the extreme Electromagnetic Interference (EMI) generated by millions of GaN switching events per second.
* **Niche Suppliers:** **Vitrek** (modular high-speed power analysis systems like the PA920 for transient analysis), **Yokogawa** (DTSX fiber-optic thermal profiling for busbars).

### Liquid Cooling CDUs (Coolant Distribution Units)
With power densities rendering air cooling thermodynamically bankrupt, CDUs are integrated directly alongside the 800VDC power shelves.
* **Specialized Suppliers:**
  * **Asetek** (Direct-to-chip cold plates and pumps).
  * **CoolIT Systems** (Enterprise DLC and CDU manufacturing).
  * **LiquidStack** (Pioneers in two-phase immersion and modular CDU deployments).
  * **Motivair** (High-capacity CDUs heavily utilized in hyperscale buildouts).
  * **Delta Electronics** (Converging power and cooling with 800VDC-native In-Row CDUs).
